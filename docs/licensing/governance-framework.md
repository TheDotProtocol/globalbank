# Governance Framework — Global Dot Bank

## Group structure (target)

```
Global Dot Bank Group
├── BVI Licensed Bank (primary deposit-taking entity) — FSC
├── Labuan Bank (DIB) — ASEAN hub — Labuan FSA
└── Future: Thailand / India subsidiaries or branches
```

## Board & committees

| Committee | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Board of Directors | Strategy, capital, risk appetite |
| Audit Committee | Financial reporting, internal audit |
| Risk Committee | Credit, market, liquidity, operational risk |
| ALCO | Asset-liability management |
| Compliance Committee | AML, regulatory change |

## Three lines of defence

1. **Business / front office** — owns customer risk at origination
2. **Compliance & risk** — policies, monitoring, AML cases (`/admin/aml-cases`)
3. **Internal audit** — independent assurance (to be appointed)

## Key officers (appoint before application)

| Role | Responsibility | Platform |
|---|---|---|
| MLRO | SAR/STR filing, AML program | `/admin/regulatory` |
| Compliance Officer | KYC, sanctions, CTR | `/admin/compliance` |
| CRO | Enterprise risk | Board reporting |
| CFO | Capital, liquidity, GL | `/admin/reconciliation` |

Update holder names in admin: `/admin/licensing`

## Policies required (document set)

- Credit policy
- AML/CFT policy
- Customer fund safeguarding policy
- IT & cyber security policy
- BCP / DR policy
- Conflicts of interest policy
- Whistleblowing policy

## Regulatory reporting calendar (post-licence)

- Monthly AML statistics
- CTR / STR as triggered
- Quarterly capital adequacy
- Annual audited financial statements
