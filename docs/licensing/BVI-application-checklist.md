# BVI Banking License — Application Checklist

**Regulator:** BVI Financial Services Commission (FSC)  
**Entity:** The Dotprotocol Co., Ltd (or dedicated BVI banking SPV — confirm with counsel)

## Corporate & ownership

- [ ] Certificate of incorporation / good standing
- [ ] Memorandum & articles of association (banking objects clause)
- [ ] Group structure chart (BVI holdco → operating bank)
- [ ] Ultimate beneficial owner (UBO) declarations (10%+)
- [ ] Fit-and-proper questionnaires for all directors & senior officers
- [ ] Police / credit checks for key persons

## Business plan

- [ ] Executive summary (5 pages)
- [ ] Target market & customer segments
- [ ] Products at launch: deposits, lending, FX, cards (roadmap)
- [ ] 3-year financial projections (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow)
- [ ] Break-even analysis

## Capital & financial resources

- [ ] Minimum capital evidence (per FSC banking guidelines)
- [ ] Source of funds declaration for shareholders
- [ ] Liquidity risk policy
- [ ] Capital adequacy monitoring framework

## Governance

- [ ] Board composition (minimum independent directors)
- [ ] Board charter & committee terms of reference
- [ ] MLRO appointment letter
- [ ] Compliance officer appointment
- [ ] Internal audit function (in-house or outsourced)

## AML / CFT

- [ ] Written AML/CFT policy (see `aml-program-outline.md`)
- [ ] Customer due diligence procedures (Sumsub integration documented)
- [ ] Sanctions screening procedures
- [ ] SAR/STR escalation to FSC
- [ ] AML training program

## Technology & operations

- [ ] Core banking architecture diagram
- [ ] GL / double-entry reconciliation process
- [ ] BCP / DR plan
- [ ] Cyber security policy
- [ ] Outsourcing register (cloud, KYC vendor, payment processors)

## Customer protection

- [ ] Terms & conditions (deposit, lending)
- [ ] Customer fund safeguarding policy
- [ ] Complaints handling procedure
- [ ] Privacy policy (GDPR / applicable law)
